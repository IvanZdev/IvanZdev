Community

Here at Xtinction PVP, we pride ourselves on being a server that is built and shaped through our communities’ needs. We hold community polls every season to ask our players what they want to see in future seasons. As our community changes, so do we. If you want the competitive aspect of the PVP experience, but wish for a server that offers more than just straight PVP, this is the server for you. Seasons are 3-4 months long on average.

We offer New Player and Post Raid Protection. Offline raid protection that boosts turret damage and structure health when you’re offline. Throughout the season, we host community events, mini games, and more. We want to build a server that you can PVP and play competitively on, while also creating a community that you can build friendships in and enjoy being a part of a good-hearted gaming community. Join us on our journey towards making Xtinction PVP a server that’s enjoyable for all.

Team

We have a large variety of admins here at Xtinction to help make your experience the best that it can be. Each of us brings a little bit of expertise in different areas to give us a well-rounded team of individuals to keep the server fresh and vibrant. Admins are very active with a team of moderators to help assist you 24/7.
